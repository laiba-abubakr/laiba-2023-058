James, his sister Sally, and Mark, their BFF (Best Friend Forever) were bored.
They didn’t know what to do and it was a little too early for them to go home.
So Sally said, “I have an idea! Let’s play Hide-and Seek.”
Mark turned facing towards the big tree, closed his eyes and started counting out loud.
Sally and James looked at each other,
and ran off in different directions to find a hiding place.
